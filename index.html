The Next Steps Accelerator Programme

The Convener, Lydia Effiong.

Learn new skills to help you get the job you want.

The Next Steps Accelerator Programme was created to provide the necessary digital and soft skills to fit into the 21st century work structure eihter in paid employment or in a bid to venture into entrepreneurship.This is done by training, providing mentorship and internship oppurtunities to hardworking participants.

Stage One: Next Steps Summit

Next Steps Summit: This platform brings together seasoned speakers across several industries, including Human Resources Experts to relate with the issues of Nigerian graduates and provide them with some guidance needed.

Stage Two: Next Steps Academy

All participants are obliged to go through digital and soft skills training for a period of 4-6 weeks with expert trainers. Completion of this training is mandatory to move to the next stage.

Stage Two: Next Steps Internship

Next Steps Internship: All students of the academy who completed their program will go through an evaluation program. This will enable us place each candidate in a suitable Organisation for his/her internship and career mentorship for a period of 2-3 months.
